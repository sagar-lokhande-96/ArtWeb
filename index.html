Sketching

Sketching is a drawing style that is usually done quickly, using loose lines. It is often used as a preliminary step to create a more finished piece of art. You can use any medium to sketch, but pencils are the most popular choice as they allow you to easily erase any mistakes. Sketching with a pen can also be fun, as it forces you to commit to your lines and makes your sketches more spontaneous. When sketching, try not to think too much about the final product, just let your pencil flow across the paper and see what happens!
